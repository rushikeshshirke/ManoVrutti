MHA Screening is an educational program intended to help inform people about options they have in getting help for mental health issues. It may suggest tools and resources that offer information, treatment services, self-help (or “DIY”) tools, and/or ways to connect with others. It does not represent its results as an exhaustive list of all services available to a given individual for a given mental health concern, as an endorsement of specific treatments or services, or as a replacement for treatment or services as performed by a qualified provider. For any and all suggestions, comments, or questions, please contact Mental Health America.
